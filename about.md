These are the included workshop materials for [Cornell HD4630: Introduction to fMRI Analysis](https://classes.cornell.edu/browse/roster/SP17/class/HD/4630), Spring 2017.

This workshops series is designed to accompany [Nathan Spreng](http://lbc.human.cornell.edu/Home.html)'s course, HD4630. It aims to teach undergraduate and graduate students functional neuroimaging data analysis with little or no expectation of previous coding experience.
